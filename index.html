About

The stereoscopic 3D photos were taken using Canon EOS R7 with RF-S3.9mm F3.5 STM Dual Fisheye by Jonghyeon Lee. These spatial photos can be viewed stereoscopically on Apple Vision Pro with visionOS 2.2 or later using Safari. Please feel free to connect with me and let me know if you have questions.
